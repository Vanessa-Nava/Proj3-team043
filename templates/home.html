iCry

Inspired by Cean Manzano, Jose Crescencio, Gabriel Sanchez



Monitoring your baby isn’t enough. As a parent, you want to know why they are crying. With this app, you’ll be able to record the last times you’ve changed your baby’s diaper, the last time you fed them, and the initial time they have fallen asleep. So once they start crying you’ll get an idea of why your baby is crying!


*We will send email alerts that will display the last event(s) you had with your baby before they started crying.
